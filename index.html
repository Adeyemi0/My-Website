Here's What I Actually Do (And Don't Do)

Most businesses don't have a data problem, the real challenge is using it to make confident, timely decisions.

You're tracking everything, website clicks, sales, support tickets, user behavior. The data exists. But when you need to decide whether to launch that new feature, double down on a marketing channel, or figure out why customers suddenly stopped buying, you're back to guessing.

I work with founders and growth teams who know their data holds answers but don't have time to become data scientists themselves. Over the past 4 years, I've helped teams predict which customers will churn 60 days out, identify which $10K marketing experiment will return $100K, and automate decisions that used to take 3 meetings and a spreadsheet.

I don't build AI for the sake of AI. I start with your business question, find the simplest path to an answer, and ship working solutions fast. You'll get models that run in production, dashboards your team actually uses, and clear documentation so you're never locked into me.
